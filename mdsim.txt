Molecular Dynamics - Simulations :h3

The next sections list papers describing various MD simulations I've
collaborated on for different kinds of materials: granular systems,
coarse-grained modesl, polymers, metals, glasses, polyelectrolytes,
silicon, and liquid crystals.

"This page"_md.html gives a brief description of what molecular
dynamics is about, and my work in developing parallel algorithms and
codes for doing MD.

Parallel MD codes I've written and distribute freely are available
from "this page"_download.html.  Simulation work of others using these
codes is listed on the "Publications
page"_https://www.lammps.org/papers.html of the "LAMMPS
website"_lammps, along with
"pictures"_https://www.lammps.org/pictures.html and
"movies"_https://www.lammps.org/movies.html.

:link(lammps,https://www.lammps.org)
